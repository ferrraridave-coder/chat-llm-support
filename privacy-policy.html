Privacy Policy for Chat LLM

Last updated: 7 December 2025

Chat LLM is developed and maintained by David Picknell. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data and what information is transmitted to external services.

1. Information You Provide

Chat LLM allows you to input:

  • Text prompts
  • Images
  • Documents (PDF, text files, etc.)

This content is never stored by the app. It is only transmitted to third-party AI model providers for processing.

2. How Your Data Is Used

When you submit a prompt, image, or document, Chat LLM securely sends your content to OpenRouter and/or OpenAI (depending on the model you select). These services process your request and return a response.

Chat LLM does not:

  • Store your data
  • Create user accounts
  • Track personal information
  • Sell or share your data

3. Data Retention by Third-Party Providers

Chat LLM does not retain any user content, but third-party AI providers have their own retention policies. Please review their documentation:

  • OpenRouter: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

By using Chat LLM, you acknowledge that data sent to these providers is handled under their respective policies.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

Chat LLM does not include:

  • Analytics tracking
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Crash reporting services

5. Security

All requests are transmitted securely using industry-standard HTTPS encryption.

6. Your Rights

Because Chat LLM does not store any personal information, there is no stored data to delete or request access to.
