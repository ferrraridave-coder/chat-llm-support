Privacy Policy for Chat LLM and LLM Pro

Last updated: 2 May 2026

Chat LLM and LLM Pro are developed and maintained by David Picknell ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how these apps handle your data and what information may be transmitted to external services when you use their features.

1. On-Device Local AI Model (Chat LLM Only)

Chat LLM offers an optional on-device AI model that runs entirely on your device. The model is not bundled with the app: it is downloaded on demand from Hugging Face the first time you tap the Local LLM card in the model picker. After this initial download, the model runs locally and works offline.

  • The download itself contacts Hugging Face to fetch the model files; no chat content is sent during this step
  • Once downloaded, all conversations remain on your device
  • No chat content is ever transmitted to any external server when using the local model
  • The app can function offline once the model is on the device
  • No account or API key is required
  • Prompts and responses are stored locally on your device only
  • You can delete the downloaded model from the model picker at any time to reclaim storage

We do not have access to any conversations you have with the local model. This feature is only available in Chat LLM. LLM Pro operates exclusively using cloud-based services.

2. Free Cloud AI Access (Groq)

Both Chat LLM and LLM Pro provide free cloud AI access without requiring an API key. When using free cloud AI:

  • Your text prompts and conversation content are sent to our backend server
  • Requests are forwarded to Groq to generate AI responses
  • Responses are returned to your device
  • No account or API key is required

We do not store your prompts or responses. Messages are processed in real time and discarded after delivery. Free cloud access is text-only and does not support image or document uploads.

3. Premium Subscription (LLM Pro Only)

LLM Pro offers a premium subscription (£9.99 per month) that unlocks additional AI features. When using premium subscription features:

  • Your prompts and conversation content are sent to supported AI providers to generate AI responses
  • Depending on the model selected, requests are sent to Groq and/or Cerebras
  • Requests may pass through our backend server where required
  • Responses are returned to your device
  • Some models may retrieve up-to-date information via web search (powered by Serper)
  • Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple's App Store

We do not store prompts, responses, or generated images. All processing is performed in real time and data is discarded after delivery.

4. AI Image Generation (LLM Pro Only) (Replicate)

LLM Pro includes built-in AI image generation and image-to-image editing. When using image generation or editing:

  • Text prompts and any selected source images are transmitted to Replicate for processing
  • Generated images are returned to your device
  • Image usage limits are enforced and displayed in-app

We do not store image prompts or generated images on our servers.

5. Spoken Playback of AI Replies (Chat LLM Only) (Replicate)

Chat LLM optionally lets you play any AI reply aloud using the Kokoro voice model hosted on Replicate. This feature requires you to supply your own Replicate API key, which is added in Settings then API Keys. The play button next to AI messages and the Voice Playback option in the menu only appear once a key has been entered. When using spoken playback:

  • The text of the selected AI message is transmitted to Replicate using your own API key
  • Replicate generates an audio file using the Kokoro voice model and returns it to your device
  • Audio is cached locally on your device so repeat playback of the same message does not re-incur a generation cost
  • Generation costs are billed to your Replicate account at Replicate's standard rate
  • Your Replicate API key is stored only on your device

We do not store message text, audio, or your API key on our servers.

6. Voice Chat

Chat LLM — Real-time Voice Chat (Google Gemini):
Chat LLM supports real-time voice conversations powered by Google Gemini. This feature requires you to supply your own Gemini API key. When using voice chat in Chat LLM:

  • Your spoken input and conversation content are transmitted to Google to generate AI responses
  • Audio is processed in real time for speech recognition and response generation
  • Any images or photos you attach during a voice session are also transmitted to Google for processing
  • Gemini may automatically perform Google Search queries to answer questions about current events, weather, or other live information
  • Conversation transcripts may be stored locally on your device
  • Your Gemini API key is stored only on your device

LLM Pro — Real-time Voice Chat (xAI):
LLM Pro optionally supports real-time voice conversations using xAI. This feature requires you to supply your own compatible API key. When using voice chat in LLM Pro:

  • Your spoken input and conversation content are transmitted to xAI to generate AI responses
  • Audio is processed in real time for speech recognition and response generation
  • Conversation transcripts may be stored locally on your device
  • Your API key is stored only on your device

Neither app stores voice data or transcripts on our servers.

7. Bring Your Own API Key (Advanced Option)

Both apps allow you to connect your own API keys to access additional third-party AI services. If you choose to use this feature:

  • Content is transmitted directly from your device to the selected provider
  • Prompts, images, and documents are handled by that provider
  • API keys are stored only on your device

This feature may include providers such as OpenRouter and OpenAI. If you use OpenRouter, OpenRouter may route requests to different model providers depending on the model selected. When OpenRouter web search is enabled in Chat LLM, search queries are processed by OpenRouter as part of the request. The query is augmented with your device's country and timezone so search results are region-relevant; no other personal information is included.

We do not receive, store, or log content sent using your own API keys.

8. How Your Data Is Used

Neither Chat LLM nor LLM Pro creates user accounts, tracks usage, or collects analytics. We do not sell or share user data with advertisers.

Neither app does the following:

  • Store prompts, images, documents, voice data, or generated audio on our servers
  • Store API keys on our servers
  • Track users across apps or websites
  • Include advertising or analytics SDKs
